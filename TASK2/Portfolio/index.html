Smart Home Energy Monitoring System

The Smart Home Energy Monitoring System is a project designed to monitor and analyze the energy consumption within a residential setting. This system utilizes electrical sensors and smart metering technology to provide real-time data on energy usage for various appliances and devices in the home. By integrating this data into a centralized platform, homeowners can gain insights into their energy consumption patterns, identify energy-saving opportunities, and make informed decisions to optimize their energy usage. The project involves the development of a user-friendly interface, data collection and analysis algorithms, and the implementation of communication protocols to enable seamless integration with the existing electrical infrastructure of a home. The Smart Home Energy Monitoring System not only promotes energy efficiency but also empowers homeowners to take control of their energy usage and contribute to a sustainable future.

Wireless Power Transfer System for Electric Vehicles

The Wireless Power Transfer System for Electric Vehicles is an innovative project that aims to revolutionize the way electric vehicles are charged. This system eliminates the need for physical cables and charging stations by utilizing wireless power transfer technology. It enables electric vehicles to charge simply by parking over a designated charging pad, which wirelessly transfers power to the vehicle's battery system. The project involves the design and implementation of efficient power transfer coils, the development of control algorithms for safe and efficient charging, and the integration of communication protocols for seamless interaction between the vehicle and the charging infrastructure. This system not only simplifies the charging process for electric vehicle owners but also reduces the need for extensive charging infrastructure deployment. By promoting convenient and efficient charging, the Wireless Power Transfer System contributes to the wider adoption of electric vehicles and the advancement of sustainable transportation solutions.
